FAQ

Common questions

Will customers know it's AI?

Ringr sounds natural and conversational. Most callers can't tell the difference. If asked directly, Ringr identifies as an AI assistant ‚Äî we believe in transparency.

What happens when I hit my minute limit?

You'll get a notification when you're close. Once you hit the limit, new calls won't be answered until your next billing cycle or until you upgrade to a higher plan.

Can I use my existing phone number?

Yes. You can port your existing number to Ringr, or forward calls from your current number to your new Ringr number.

What if the AI can't handle a call?

Ringr can transfer calls to you or take a message. You set the rules for when to escalate, and you can always review recordings and transcripts.

Is there a contract?

No contracts, no commitments. Pay monthly and cancel anytime. We keep customers by being useful, not by locking you in.

What industries does Ringr work for?

Any service business that books appointments by phone: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, landscaping, cleaning, home services, and more.
